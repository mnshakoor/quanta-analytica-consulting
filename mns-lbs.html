Process

How I Work

Rigor made visible through process
1

Define the Decision Problem

Clarify the decision, constraints, stakeholders, and what must be true for success. Restate the problem until it becomes actionable.

2

Test Competing Explanations

Build a hypothesis set, document assumptions, and track what is unknown. Reduce uncertainty through structured questioning and validation.

3

Install Indicators and Thresholds

Translate uncertainty into observable signals. Define triggers for escalation, mitigation, and leadership decision points.

4

Deliver Decision-Ready Outputs

Produce briefs, SitReps, and risk registers with confidence statements and monitoring logic aligned to operational tempo.

Core Thesis

What the essay explores

The Human Rupture is framed as an exploration of a widening civilizational mismatch: humanity's material and technological capabilities are advancing faster than the civic, ethical, and moral consciousness required to govern them responsibly.

Author and associated organizations

About the Author

Johan Obdola

Johan Obdola is presented here as a strategic analyst and author working at the intersection of global security, institutional decay, and the human condition. His body of work spans visible crisis systems such as organized crime and narcoterrorism while also probing deeper fractures of consciousness that allow such crises to endure.

He is identified with a dual focus: diagnosing overt security breakdowns while also examining the moral and civic deficits that undermine coherent response in periods of systemic transformation.

He is also noted as the author of the Dangerous Times and Narcoterrorism 3.0 book series.

GDELT Query Reference Official Docs ↗
Boolean Operators
term1 term2

Implicit AND — both terms must appear.

term1 OR term2

Either term must appear (OR must be uppercase).

-term

Exclude articles containing this term (NOT).

"exact phrase"

Match the exact multi-word phrase.

(term1 OR term2)

Grouping for complex boolean logic.

near10:term1 term2

Both terms within 10 words of each other.

Field Filters
domain:reuters.com

Restrict to articles from a specific domain.

domainis:reuters.com

Exact domain match (no subdomains).

sourcelang:English

Filter by article source language.

sourcecountry:US

Filter by source country FIPS code (US, UK, SU…).

tone>5

Articles with tone score above 5 (positive).

tone<-5

Articles with tone score below −5 (negative).

image:yes

Articles containing at least one image.

video:yes

Articles containing embedded video.

Output Modes (mode=)
artlist

Returns list of matching articles (title, URL, date, tone).

timelinevol

Volume of coverage over time (normalised).

timelinevolraw

Raw article count over time.

timelinelang

Coverage volume broken down by language.

timelinesourcecountry

Coverage broken down by source country.

tonechart

Distribution of tone scores across results.

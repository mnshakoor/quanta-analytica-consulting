03 // ANALYTIC PERSPECTIVE
What the spreadsheet actually contains
ANALYTIC PERSPECTIVE
The workbook is not just a demographic list. It is structured as a QAP-ready identity layer dataset designed to support fragility modeling, conflict mapping, and behavioral risk assessment.
01
Master Reference

Indexed ethnolinguistic groups with analytic notes. Includes identity clusters relevant to political mobilization. Contains structured IDs for cross-sheet linking.

02
Language Families

Macro-level linguistic structure across Africa. Useful for identifying civilizational or historical identity blocks. Includes analytic significance notes tied to geopolitical influence patterns.

03
Country–Group Index

Maps states to major ethnolinguistic populations. Highlights identity complexity and fragmentation risk. Supports country-level stability modeling.

04
Conflict Relevance Index

Pre-identified identity groups with relevance to instability pathways. Includes Indicators and Warnings notes aligned to QAP analytic logic. Links identity structure to operational risk environments.

05
Source & Metadata

Traceability layer documenting methodology and cartographic source extraction. Supports auditability requirements under QAP WSI tradecraft standards.

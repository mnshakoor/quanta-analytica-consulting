03 // ABOUT
About This Portal

The Quanta Analytica Resource Portal provides centralised access to the full suite of analytical tools, data platforms, and intelligence products developed under the QAP™ framework by Quanta Analytica and MNS Consulting.

All resources are produced in accordance with the Quanta Analytica Process™ (QAP™) — a framework-governed, LLM-augmented intelligence and risk analysis methodology. Each tool integrates structured analytic tradecraft, open-source data, and domain-specific risk modelling to support decision-ready intelligence outputs.

For full methodology documentation, visit quanta-analytica.com. For support or to commission a bespoke assessment, contact MNS Consulting directly.

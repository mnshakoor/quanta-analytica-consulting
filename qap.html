Methodology

The Four-Stage Process

Every QAP™ deliverable moves through the same rigorous pipeline — no shortcuts, no exceptions.

01
Data Ingestion & Framing

Raw information is structured through empirical analytic frameworks, eliminating noise and establishing a clear problem frame before any modeling begins.

02
LLM-Augmented Analysis

Large language models apply structured reasoning under strict governance protocols, generating candidate assessments and scenario branches across the framed problem set.

03
Expert Validation

Human analysts review, stress-test, and refine every output. WSI™ source auditing and confidence banding are applied. No product advances without sign-off.

04
Decision-Grade Deliverable

A final, stakeholder-ready product: clear, reproducible, and precisely calibrated for the specific decision environment it serves.
